Tianyi Yu

    I am a CRM-ISM postdoc at Université du Québec à Montréal. I got my Ph.D. in 2024 from the University of California, San Diego, where my advisor was Brendon Rhoades. My research is about algebraic combinatorics. Before that, I obtained bachelor's degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of California, San Diego. My email address is yu.tianyi (at) uqam.ca. My CV is here.
